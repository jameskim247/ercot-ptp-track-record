# Weekly Reports

Weekly delayed aggregate reports are generated here for the previous calendar week after at least five settlement-complete valid outcome days are available.

If the threshold is not met, no weekly report is published for that period. Skipped reports are preferable to discretionary partial-period reporting.

## Superseded Reports

`reports/weekly/2026-06-09_to_2026-06-13.md` was generated by a prior automation defect that used a 5-day Tuesday-Saturday window instead of the previous calendar week. It is superseded by `reports/weekly/2026-06-08_to_2026-06-14.md`, the canonical Monday-Sunday week, which adds the omitted Sunday 2026-06-14. The five overlapping delivery days (2026-06-09..2026-06-13) are identical; do not double-count. See `releases/superseded_reports.csv`.
