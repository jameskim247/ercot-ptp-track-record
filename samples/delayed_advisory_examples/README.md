# Delayed Advisory Examples

Only long-delayed, sanitized examples may be published here.

Do not publish same-day actionable recommendations or raw scored signals.

This directory is not populated by default. A sample may be added only after all of these are true:

- The sample is long-delayed and no longer actionable.
- The sample has explicit approval recorded outside this public repository.
- The sample contains no raw source/sink/MW rows, scored-signal rows, model internals, or private paths.
- The public verifier passes before publication.

Aggregate counts and hashes are preferred over row-level advisory examples.
