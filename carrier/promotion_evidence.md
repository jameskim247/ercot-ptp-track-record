# Promotion Evidence

Promotion evidence for the current public carrier code is maintained privately until it is safe and appropriate to disclose under NDA.

Public reports in this repository must rely on prospective public ledger rows and delayed aggregate outcomes.

Public promotion evidence is populated only from prospective public rows:

- Preliminary public evidence may be summarized after at least 30 valid prospective days.
- Stronger public evidence may be summarized after 60 to 90 valid prospective days.
- Mature public evidence may be summarized after 180 or more valid prospective days.

Any public promotion summary must reference delayed aggregate reports and must not include baseline-comparison metrics, raw advisories, source/sink/MW rows, model internals, or private diligence artifacts.
